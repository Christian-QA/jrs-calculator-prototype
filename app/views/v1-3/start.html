Coronavirus Job Retention Scheme calculator

Employers can make a claim through the Job Retention Scheme if they have put their employees on temporary leave (furlough) because of the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

Use this calculator to work out the figures you’ll need when you complete an online claim through the scheme.

The calculator is aimed at organisations with a small number of employees

How it works

The calculator will tell you what to claim for:

  • wages
  • employer's National Insurance contributions
  • employer's pension contributions

It will also give you a breakdown of the calculations for each pay date.

The figures are for one employee, for the claim period you specify. Run the calculator again for any other employees you’re claiming for and add up the results.

Start now

Before you start

You will need the employee’s:

  • most recent pay dates (covering the claim period)
  • how much they earn
  • The date their furlough started (and ended, if not ongoing)
  • National Insurance category letter
  • pension auto-enrolment status (whether or not they opted out)
