Coronavirus Job Retention Scheme calculator

Employers can make a claim through the Job Retention Scheme if they have put their employees on temporary leave (furlough) because of the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

Use this calculator to help you work out the figures you’ll need to complete an online claim through the scheme.

The calculator is aimed at organisations with a small number of employees.

How it works

The calculator will work out what you can claim for wages, employer's National Insurance contributions and employer's pension contributions for one employee for the claim period you specify.

It will also give you a breakdown of the calculations for each pay date - keep this for your records.

You’ll need to run the calculator for each employee and combine the totals.

Start now

Before you start

You will need the employee’s

  • most recent pay dates (covering the claim period)
  • period of furlough
  • National Insurance category number
  • pension auto-enrolment status (whether or not they opted out)
