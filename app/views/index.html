JRS Calculator

Prototype of the calculator to support the JRS scheme

Version 1.3

Changes since previous version:

  • Added variable/less than 12 month employed journey
  • Changed order of frequency and regular/varialbe amount
  • Added date capture at the end of the journey if the pay period spans the tax year
  • Added NIC category info
  • Separated the claim start and end date to two screens
  • Simplified the furlough question and moved it in the order
  • Continue to ask for pay dates until you get to the one after the end of the claim date

Assumptions for the flow:

  • A calculation for the whole claim period
  • The claim period covers transition of tax years
  • The employee is payed the same each time
  • The employee has been furloughed for the whole claim period
