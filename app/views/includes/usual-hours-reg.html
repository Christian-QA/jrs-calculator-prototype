Work out your employee’s usual hours

For an employee on fixed hours whose pay does not depend on hours worked:

  1. Start with the hours this employee was contracted for at the end of the last pay period ending before 20 March.
  2. Divide by the number of calendar days in the repeating working pattern, including non-working days.
  3. Multiply by the number of calendar days in the pay period (or partial pay period) you’re claiming for.
  4. Round up or down if the result isn’t a whole number.

If this employee was sick, on annual leave, or on family-related statutory leave during the last pay period ending before 20 March, work out their usual hours as if they had not taken that leave.
