What is a claim period?

You make a claim under the Job Retention Scheme for a period during which one or more of your employees are furloughed. This is the claim period.

Include all affected employees in one claim, including employees who start or end furlough on different dates within the claim period.

You cannot make more than one claim covering the same claim period.

Claim period start date

For the first claim, this is the date the first employee was furloughed (other employees may have been furloughed at a later date). The earliest start date was 1 March 2020, which was the date the Job Retention Scheme started.

If employees are still on furlough or flexible furlough after a claim period ends, make another claim later to cover the ongoing furlough period. Claim periods cannot overlap.
