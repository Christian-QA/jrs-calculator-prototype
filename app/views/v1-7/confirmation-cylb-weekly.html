Detailed breakdown of calculations

You told us your employee gets paid a variable amount each time and has worked for you for more than 12 months. There are 2 methods we can use to work out pay. We’ve used the one that gave the higher figure. The furlough grant is 80% of this.

Method 1: we’ve taken the amounts they were paid for equivalent pay periods in 2019 and applied this to the number of furlough days in each pay period.

Method 2: we’ve worked out their average daily earnings in the last tax year, by dividing their total pay by the number of calendar days between 6 April 2019 and the day before furlough (or 5 April 2020 if earlier). Then we’ve multiplied that by the number of furlough days in each pay period.

There’s a maximum amount you can claim for furlough grant and Employer National Insurance contributions. If this affects your claim, we’ve adjusted the calculations.

Calculations are rounded to the nearest penny, unless otherwise stated.

For pay period 8 April to 15 April 2020

Calculate pay based on furlough days

Take pay from same dates last year.

Last year’s dates overlap two pay periods so in period one:

  1. Start with £550.77 (from pay day 9 April last year).
  2. Divide by 7 (days in pay period).
  3. Multiply by 2 (furlough days).

Total for period one = £157.14

Then in period two:

  1. Start with £600 (from pay day 14 April last year).
  2. Divide by 7 (days in pay period).
  3. Multiply by 5 (furlough days).

Total for period two = £428.57

Then:

  1. Add £157.14 + £428.57 (the totals from both pay periods).

Total pay from same dates last year = £585.71

Take the total pay from employee start date (or 6 April 2019, if earlier) to day before the employee's furlough start date (or 5 April 2020, if earlier):

  1. Start with £26000 (total pay).
  2. Divide by 333 (days employed) .
  3. Multiply by 5 (furlough days).

Total average pay last year = £546.46

Then compare these figures and use the higher of:

  • £585.71 (pay from same dates last year).
  • £546.46 (average pay last year).

Total pay based on furlough days = £585.71

Furlough grant

  1. Take £585.71 (pay based on furlough days).
  2. Multiply by 80%.

Total furlough grant for pay period = £456.92

  8. Divide by 7 (days in pay period).
